How does Odyssi work offline?
We cache the UI and store your notes/waypoints locally. When you sync, only encrypted blobs move.
What data do you collect?
By default: none. No cookies, no pixels, no analytics. You opt in to anything else.
Can I export my data?
Yes. You’ll be able to export to JSON/GPX/ZIP and move it wherever you like.
